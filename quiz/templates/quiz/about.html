About Us

Quizng.com is a modern web-app whose sole purpose is to provide a platform on which students (of any level) can practice questions of their chosen category.

Our user interface is simple enough for anyone to understand. From signup, to login to viewing your dashboard, and finally the quiz process. Everything is simple.

Quizng is an opensource project which is free for everyone to use and contribute to

However, while Quizng is free for everyone, we need also to finance this project in order to continue to deliver high quality service to our users.
